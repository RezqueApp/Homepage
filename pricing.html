Frequently asked questions

What countries do you support?

We can send messages to and from almost every country. However, dedicated phone numbers are limited to the United States and Canada. We can assign a phone number for most major countries but pricing will vary.

Are there any hidden fees?

There is a surcharge for messages sent outside of the following countries: US, CA, UK, or SG. In addition, usage over plan limits will incur an additional charge as well. Contact us for country based pricing.

What if I decide I want a different plan?

You can always upgrade your plan if you detect that your usage is exceeding your plan's allowance limits.

Why is Rezque so expensive?

Believe it or not, telecommunication companies still charge for SMS. Although many of us enjoy unlimited SMS mobile plans, usage is low enough for carriers to package it into an unlimited plan. You'll find that you send a lot more SMS when automating your messages.

What are my payment options?

We accept credit card and all payments are in USD. Depending on your needs and the package that you select, alternative payment options might be available.

Are there contracts or commitments?

There is no contract unless you would like to subscribe to the yearly plan, in which case, your company will receive a discount and your account will be valid for the entirety of that period.

Do I need to enter my credit card to get a free trial?

You don't need to enter your credit card until your trial has expired. If you like the service and want to continue using it, you will need to add your credit card.

What counts as an SMS?

An SMS is a set of 160 7-bit charcters. Any set of characters over that is considered to be an additional SMS.
